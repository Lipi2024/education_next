Ayodhya Ram Mandir Opening

Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Friday began 11-day-long fast ahead of 'pranpratishtha' of Ram Lalla in Ayodhya. The consecration ceremony of the new idol of Ram Lalla at the sanctum sanctorum of the Ram Mandir is scheduled for January 22. The ceremony is set to be performed by PM Modi. The live broadcast of the celebrations in Ayodhya on January 22 will be disseminated worldwide, helping people participate in the ceremony from across the globe.
